About Us: Vision & Mission

Loyola College Vettavalam is dedicated to providing quality higher education that prepares students for leadership roles in a global society. Our vision is to be a recognized center of excellence in teaching, research, and outreach. We commit to fostering an inclusive and innovative environment that encourages critical thinking and ethical practice.

Self Study Report (SSR)

The Self Study Report (SSR) details the comprehensive performance of the institution across all key areas of quality assessment. This document is crucial for external accreditation and internal quality improvement planning.

IQAC: NIRF Ranking Data

The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) data submission is managed by the IQAC. We strive for continuous improvement in all ranking parameters, including Teaching, Learning, and Resources (TLR), Research and Professional Practice (RP), and Graduation Outcomes (GO).

IQAC: NAAC Accreditation Status

Our institution currently holds an A++ accreditation status from NAAC. The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) coordinates all activities related to sustaining and enhancing quality as mandated by NAAC guidelines.
